Green Standards

Bring environmental friendly standards into local businesses.

Dispose plastic waste in appropriate place by implementing green standards in coffee shops and restaurants:
1. Make recycling bins readily available
2. Use 100% recycable cups,straws,papers, bags,etc.
3. Offer incentives or discounts for guests who bring their own mugs or recycle tumbler.
4. Encourage customers and colleagues to recycle
5. Hastag #savien on social media.
